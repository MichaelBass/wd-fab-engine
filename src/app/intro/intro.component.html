You will be asked how much you agree with statements about how you function or feel about doing different activities.

  1. Choose your answer based on your usual ability, by yourself without the help of another person, and with any equipment or devices you normally use.
  2. Do your best to pick the answer that best matches your agreement with the satement. If you are unable to pick a response the matches, choose "I don't know".

You will also be asked about how much difficulty you have doing different things.

  1. Choose your answer based on your usual ability using any equipment or devices you normally use.
  2. Choose your answer based on your ability to do the activity by yourself without the help of another person.
  3. If you have not done an activity, do your best to guess what your ability would be if you did it.
    If you are unable to gues choose "I don't know".

For example, if it takes you a very long time or it is hard for you to do something (or an activity), you would choose the answer "with a lot of difficulty". But, if you need help from another person, you would choose "Unable".
